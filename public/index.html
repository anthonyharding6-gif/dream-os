The Story

Built from the ground up.

Dream Hospitality Group was founded by George Karavias, a Greek-American with 18 years in New York nightlife. What started as a promotional outfit in 2015 has grown into an empire spanning nightclubs, restaurant concepts, and a cannabis brand.

From Harbor to Sei Less, from Gyro City to Dank by Definition, every concept is built on intentionality, quality, and a genuine love for what New York City can be.

George Karavias, Founder and President

The Story

Built from
the ground up.

Dream Hospitality Group was founded by George Karavias — a Greek-American with 18+ years in New York nightlife. What started as a promotional outfit in 2015 has grown into an empire: four nightclubs, four restaurant concepts, a cannabis brand, and a team that has redefined what hospitality means in this city.

From Harbor to Sei Less, from Gyro City to Dank by Definition — every concept is built on the same foundation: intentionality, quality, and a genuine love for what New York City can be when the right people are in the room.

— George Karavias, Founder & President
